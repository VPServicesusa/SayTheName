<section id="features"><div class="wrap">
  <div class="caps">On the road</div>
  <h2>Provision, decide, and keep them alive</h2>
  <p>Name your party and outfit them at a shop that will not let you leave unready. Then set a pace, ration the food, and answer what the road puts in front of you — hundreds of events with no obviously right answer. When someone dies, you write what goes on the stone.</p>

  <div class="grid">
    <div><h3>Ten distinct roads</h3><p>Not ten skins on one game. Each has its own economy, its own clock, and its own way of going wrong.</p></div>
    <div><h3>People, not inventory</h3><p>Everyone has a trait, someone they're close to, and a fate written after the journey ends — whether or not they finish it.</p></div>
    <div><h3>A graveyard that persists</h3><p>Names, dates, and the epitaphs you wrote carry across every run. The records remember what the runs cost.</p></div>
    <div><h3>Grit</h3><p>Spend it to face a bad roll again and get a genuinely new outcome. It's precious. Bank it for the moments that matter.</p></div>
    <div><h3>128 feats</h3><p>Some reward arriving whole. Some reward losing well. A few you will not see coming.</p></div>
    <div><h3>No ads, no purchases</h3><p>The whole game, start to finish. No account, no tracking, and nothing to buy.</p></div>
  </div>
</div></section>

<section id="faq"><div class="wrap">
  <div class="caps">FAQ</div>
  <h2>Questions, answered</h2>

  <details><summary>How do I play?</summary><p>Name your party, outfit them at the shop, then travel. Each leg you set a pace and rations and answer whatever the road brings. Watch the clock at the top — every road has one, and it is always running out.</p></details>

  <details><summary>Am I supposed to win the first time?</summary><p>No. The roads are hard in different ways and losing well is part of it. Feats, records, and the graveyard all carry across runs, so nothing is wasted.</p></details>

  <details><summary>Is there a daily challenge?</summary><p>Yes. Each day everyone gets the same road and the same seed, so the run is identical for every player. Scores are kept on your device in the current release.</p></details>

  <details><summary>Can I play without the internet?</summary><p>Yes — every part of the game works offline. The first launch with a connection loads its display fonts for nicer type, but the roads, events, saves, and sound all run with no connection at all.</p></details>

  <details><summary>How is my progress saved?</summary><p>Everything is stored locally on your device — runs in progress, career records, the graveyard, feats, and settings. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.</p></details>

  <details><summary>How do I reset my progress?</summary><p>Use the reset option in the records screen for a clean start, or delete the app to remove all of its local data.</p></details>

  <details><summary>Are there ads or in-app purchases?</summary><p>No. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscription — the whole game, start to finish.</p></details>

  <details><summary>What ages is it for?</summary><p>Teens and up. There's no graphic content, but the game deals plainly with illness, hardship, death, and choices that cost people something.</p></details>

  <details><summary>What devices does it run on?</summary><p>iPhone and iPad running iOS 14 or later. The game plays in portrait.</p></details>
</div></section>

<section id="privacy"><div class="wrap">
  <div class="caps">Privacy Policy</div>
  <h2>Your data stays on your device</h2>
  <p><em>Last updated: July 2026</em></p>
  <p>Say the Names ("the App") is a locally-stored, device-only application. The App does not collect, transmit, store, sell, or share any personal data with any party.</p>

  <h3>What we store locally</h3>
  <p>The App uses your device's local storage to save your journeys in progress and career records; the graveyard, including any names and epitaphs you enter; your feats and statistics; and your in-app preferences such as sound and volume. This data stays on your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and the developer has no access to it.</p>

  <h3>Names you enter</h3>
  <p>Party names and epitaphs are yours to choose and are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded or shared.</p>

  <h3>No third-party services</h3>
  <p>The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services. It loads display fonts from Google Fonts on first launch when a connection is available; no personal data is sent in doing so, and the App works fully without it.</p>

  <h3>No purchases</h3>
  <p>The App contains no in-app purchases and no real-money transactions.</p>

  <h3>Children</h3>
  <p>The App does not collect any personal data from users of any age, and so complies with COPPA and similar laws by not collecting anything in the first place.</p>

  <h3>Data deletion</h3>
  <p>All App data is stored on your device. To remove everything, delete the App — iOS will remove all of its associated data.</p>
